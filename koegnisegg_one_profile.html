Koegnisegg One:1

The Koenigsegg One:1 is a groundbreaking hypercar that redefined automotive performance by achieving a true 1:1 power-to-weight ratio — 1 megawatt of power for just 1,360 kg of weight. Produced in an ultra-limited run of only seven units, the One:1 represents the pinnacle of Koenigsegg’s engineering, blending extreme aerodynamics, lightweight carbon fiber construction, and a monstrous 5.0L twin-turbo V8 engine. Designed to dominate both the track and the road, the One:1 delivers brutal acceleration, record-setting lap times, and a driving experience unlike anything else on the planet.
