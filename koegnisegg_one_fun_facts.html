Fun Facts

  • The World’s First Megacar – The first production car to ever produce one megawatt of power.
  • Perfect 1:1 Ratio – 1,360 PS for 1,360 kg, giving unmatched balance and performance.
  • Track Record Holder – Set multiple 0–300–0 km/h and lap time records worldwide.
  • Carbon Fiber Innovation – Wheels, intake manifold, and even springs made from carbon fiber.
  • Massive Downforce – Generates over 600 kg of downforce at 260 km/h for extreme cornering grip.
  • Ultra-Rare Production – Only seven units ever built, making it a true collector’s dream.
