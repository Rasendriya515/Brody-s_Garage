Fun Facts

  • Global Debut – First unveiled at the 2017 Frankfurt Motor Show.
  • Live Telemetry Tech – Equipped with real-time telemetry systems for live race data monitoring.
  • Purpose-Built Race Car – Based on the BMW 8 Series but shares very few components with the road car.
  • Endurance Proven – Engineered to run flat-out for 24 hours in top-level endurance racing events.
  • Iconic BMW M Livery – The blue, dark blue (originally violet), and red stripes make it one of the most recognizable cars on the GTE grid.
